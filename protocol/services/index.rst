Services
========

The services of :ref:`Ad Select Modules <protocol-definitions-asm>` and :ref:`Ad Pay Modules <protocol-definitions-apm>` 
can be integrated within :ref:`Supply-Side Platform <protocol-definitions-ssp>` or :ref:`Demand-Side Platform <protocol-definitions-dsp>`,
or they can be externalized and delivered by third-party :ref:`Software Providers <protocol-definitions-softwareprovider>`.
